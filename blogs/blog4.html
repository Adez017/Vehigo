What’s Required When Renting a Car?

By Ravi Khanna | May 2, 2022

Renting a car for the first time? Whether it's a road trip, a business visit, or a weekend getaway, renting is easy and efficient — if you know what’s needed.

Here’s a checklist of the basic requirements when renting a vehicle through VehiGo:

  • Valid Driver’s License: Must be government-issued and at least 1 year old.
  • Age Requirement: You must be at least 21 years old. Some premium cars may require you to be 25+.
  • Government ID: A valid Aadhaar card, passport, or PAN card for verification.
  • Payment Method: A credit/debit card or UPI for booking and deposit.
  • Security Deposit: Refundable deposit varies by car type.

Additionally, it’s advisable to carry a copy of your rental agreement, review insurance coverage, and inspect the car condition during pickup.
